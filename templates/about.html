Spotify Collage

Spotify Collage is a project made for the nwHacks 2022 Hackathon. The purpose of the website is to showcase global and personal spotify statistics through a collage of songs and album covers. This website shows data for artist top tracks as well as recommendations based on the chosen genre. Additional features include showing user's current playing music, most listened artists and playlists.
