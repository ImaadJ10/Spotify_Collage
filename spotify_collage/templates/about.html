About Our Project


Spotify Collage is a project made for the nwHacks 2022 Hackathon. The purpose of the website is to showcase global spotify popularity statistics through a collage of songs and album covers. The website shows data for artist top ten tracks as well as the current global top fifty trending music. Additional features include showing user's current playing music, liked songs and recommendation based on chosen genre.

Project created by Imaad Junaidi, Yajas Malhotra, Eric Li, Yu Cheng Li
