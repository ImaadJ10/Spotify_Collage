About SpotifyCollage


SpotifyCollage was made for the nwHacks 2022 Hackathon. With SpotifyCollage, users have access to a variety of data (made available through the Spotify Web API) all bundled together in aesthetic collages! You can take a look at your 50 most recent liked songs, the top trending songs, your favorite artist's greatest hits, as well as recommendations from multiple genres! SpotifyCollage offers the functionality of Spotify with a more compact, stylish look, and lets users find what they need, as soon as they need it.


Project created by Imaad Junaidi, Yajas Malhotra, Eric Li, Yu Cheng Li
